[1611100] Disclosure of Notes to the financial statements - Property, Plant and Equipment - General Industry

Pengungkapan Disclosure
31 December 2022
Pengungkapan catatan atas aset tetap As of 31 December 2022 and 2021, management has reviewed the estimated useful lives of fixed assets and has found them to be appropriate. The useful lives are based on the estimated period over which future economic benefits will be received by the Group, taking into account any unexpected changes in circumstances or events. As of 31 December 2022 and 2021, majority of fixed assets were insured for Rp 346,000,000,000. Management assesses that the sum insured can cover the risk of potential loss due to fire, natural disaster, strike, riot and civil commotion, etc. The Group owns several parcels of land located in Cileungsi, Karawang and Gresik with renewable Building Use Rights (Hak Guna Bangunan or HGB) due between 2023 and 2037. Management anticipates that the usage right granted under this certificate will be perpetually renewable at minimal cost. As of 31 December 2022, the acquisition cost of fully depreciable assets that were still being used amounted to Rp 690,655,163,428. Following the closing of Cileungsi office and factory and part of Karawang factory (see notes 1a and 1d), management assessed conditions of all related building improvements, machinery and equipment, office equipment and assets under constructions. As of 31 December 2022, impairment provision of Rp 255,218,589,042 was provided for all assets that no longer can be used and the net book value become zero. The remaining assets will be sold/disposed in the near future. As of 31 December 2022, balances amounting to Rp 6,329,105,655 remained unpaid for purchase of certain fixed assets (2021: Rp 1,548,607,370). As of 31 December 2022, the fair value of the Group?s fixed assets (land, buildings and improvement, machinery and equipment) amounted to Rp 1,381,451,512,000.

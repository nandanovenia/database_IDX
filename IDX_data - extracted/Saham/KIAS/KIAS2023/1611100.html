Pengungkapan catatan atas aset tetap As of 31 December 2023 and 2022, management has reviewed the estimated useful lives of fixed assets and has found them to be appropriate. The useful lives are based on the estimated period over which future economic benefits will be received by the Group, taking into account any unexpected changes in circumstances or events. As of 31 December 2023 and 2022, majority of fixed assets were insured for Rp 346,000,000,000. Management assesses that the sum insured can cover the risk of potential loss due to fire, natural disaster, strike, riot and civil commotion, etc. The Group owns several parcels of land located in Cileungsi, Karawang and Gresik with renewable Building Use Rights (Hak Guna Bangunan or HGB) due between 2024 and 2043. Management anticipates that the usage right granted under this certificate will be perpetually renewable at minimal cost. As of 31 December 2023, the acquisition cost of fully depreciable assets that were still being used amounted to Rp 513,484,028,164. Following the closing of Cileungsi office and factory and part of Karawang factory (see notes 1a and 1d), management assessed conditions of all related building improvements, machinery and equipment, office equipment and assets under constructions. As of 31 December 2023, assets impairment of Rp 101,943,399,107 was provided for all assets that no longer can be used. The remaining assets will be sold/disposed in the near future. As of 31 December 2023, balances amounting to Rp 2,794,506,208 remained unpaid for purchase of certain fixed assets (2022: Rp 6,329,105,655).

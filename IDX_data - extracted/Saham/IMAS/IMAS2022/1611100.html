Manajemen berpendapat bahwa nilai tercatat semua aset Grup dapat terealisasi seluruhnya, dan oleh karena itu, tidak diperlukan penyisihan penurunan nilai aset. In compliance with SFAS No. 48, ?Impairment of Asset Value?, asset values are reviewed for any impairment and possible write-down of carrying values whenever events or changes in circumstances indic?ate that their carrying values may not be fully recoverable.
